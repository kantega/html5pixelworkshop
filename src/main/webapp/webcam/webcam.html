Webcam selfie stick

In this task you will use the getUserMedia API to connect your laptop's webcam to a video element. If you are using Chrome you have to enable the flag chrome://flags/#enable-experimental-web-platform-features to turn on the API.

Your goal is to:

  • Use the getUserMedia function to request a video stream from your webcam
  • In the success function: Use URL.createObjectURL to create a URL from the stream
  • Set the video's src attribute to this URL
  • Again, Google is your friend

Once you've got your video connected, you can:

  • Change the function animationLoop such that your video will play on the phone's screen
